Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

🔭 Our Vision

To see every believer in Christ fully equipped, deeply rooted in the Word, and actively engaged in the ministry of the Kingdom of God in their generation.

🎯 Our Mission

To equip the saints for the work of the ministry through sound teaching, discipleship, prayer, and fellowship — so that the body of Christ may be built up in love.

📖 Our Values

Biblical authority · Spirit-filled living · Christ-centred ministry · Unity in the body · Servant leadership · Prayer and intercession.

🌍 Our Reach

Based in Nakuru, Kenya, Rhema Ministries Embassy serves believers locally and supports the wider body of Christ through resources, teaching, and ministry partnerships.
