Our Mission

Equipping Believers
for God's Kingdom

Rhema Ministries Embassy is a ministry devoted to building up the body of Christ through sound biblical teaching, discipleship, and equipping every believer for active service in the work of the Kingdom.

We are grounded in the conviction that every saint is called to minister — and it is our joy and purpose to help them discover, develop, and deploy their God-given gifts.

📖
Sound Teaching

Rooted in the inspired, infallible Word of God as the sole authority for faith and life.

🔥
Spirit-Led

Empowered by the Holy Spirit to live godly lives and minister with boldness and grace.

✝️
Christ-Centred

Jesus Christ — His life, death, resurrection, and return — is the heart of everything we do.

🤝
Unity in the Body

Celebrating the spiritual unity of all believers as one Body in our Lord Jesus Christ.

What We Believe

Statement of Faith

01

We believe the Bible to be the inspired, the only infallible, authoritative Word of God.

2 Timothy 3:16–17
02

We believe there is only one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son and Holy Spirit.

Matthew 28:19
03

We believe in the deity of Christ, His virgin birth, His sinless life, His miracles, His atoning death, His bodily resurrection, His ascension, and His personal return in power and glory.

John 1:1 · Matthew 1:18 · Hebrews 9:27–28
04

We believe in the salvation of the lost and that regeneration by the Holy Spirit is absolutely essential.

John 3:16 · Titus 3:3–7
05

We believe in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit, by whose indwelling the believer is enabled to live a godly life.

John 14:15–26 · Ephesians 1:13–14
06

We believe in the resurrection of both believers and non-believers — believers unto eternal life, and non-believers unto damnation.

Matthew 25:31–46 · 1 Thessalonians 4:13–18
07

We believe in the spiritual unity of all believers in our Lord Jesus Christ as the Body of Christ.

Philippians 2:1–4 · 1 Corinthians 12:27
